Single Sign on (SSO)

Your employees, customers and partners want seamless access to your resources from any platform and to share data with mobile, cloud and enterprise applications.

The security concern is obvious. By duplicate accounts, passwords, VPNs and multiple sign-ons, security is not being improved. Infact, these complications can adversely affect productivity.

Single sign-on (SSO) allows users to sign on once, using one set of secure corporate credentials, giving them one-click access to all applications from anywhere.

Even better, SSO will save you both time and money and free support staff from the rigours of constantly managing password resets.
