About

EA-Optimised was founded in 2011 by Iain Cox, to provide in-depth Enterprise and Solutions Architecture. During the intervening years, we have worked on many Digital Transformations, Mobile and Green-Field projects.

Engaging across a wide section of industry, we primarily focus on Banking, FinTech and the Public Sector. Throughout this journey, two things have become clear; firstly, increasingly, Businesses are moving to “The Cloud”. Secondly, Security is rightly the single issue that today concerns business owners and directors above all other.

Consequently, since 2015, EA-Optimised has specialised in Cloud Migration and Identity and Access Management (IAM), working with organisations such as HSBC, One Family Mutual, The Home Office and Welsh Government.

Our engagements can be anything from an IAM “Go Live Readiness Assessment”, to a complete bespoke enterprise/ cloud and security architecture lasting 18 months or more.

Today, EA-Optimised is partnered with Ping Identity, ForgeRock and Callsign to delver the very best IAM and Cloud experience.
